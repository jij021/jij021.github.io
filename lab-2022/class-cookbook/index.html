Dinner: Plan B

By Janice Ji

No comments

A dinner for when going out just isn't optimal. Unlike "Dinner: Plan A," this recipe can be done in 10 minutes at minimum (or never at maximum, depending on who you're eating with, so choose your ingredients wisely).

Ingredients

  • 1 person to eat with
  • A handful of cash
  • A car optional
  • Internet connection
  • 1 screen size may vary
  • Streaming service of choice
  • Plenty of time

Instructions

Prep work:

  1. Set yourself and the other person in a warm room together to let yourselves mingle and figure out what to get for dinner. See notes for more details.

Main course:

  1. Order the meal online. Locate the establishment on a phone for later.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare a show or movie to watch. Finely comb through the selection available.
  3. Once meal is ready, quickly grab car keys to pick the food up. If establishment is within walking distance, skip this step.
  4. Bring the food back home or grab your food from the delivery person. Wash hands thoroughly.
  5. Allow the food to cool on the table as the conversation with your friend gets carried away. Skip this step if you're starving.
  6. Turn TV on and put the volume on high for a more immersive experience.
  7. For the best experience, garnish your meal by commentating on whatever you're watching with your dining companion.

Notes:

If figuring out what to eat takes longer than 10 minutes, just get pizza.
