⬤   For much of history, the sky has fascinated us to have been the subject of countless works and studies. Using p5.js, this site holds 50 randomly generated iterations of what a sky could look like through a more digital lens. Make new color palettes and compositions by clicking the button below once it reads Generate new skies.

This shows the canvas in motion, simulating how a sky could be represented through code. The color scheme is randomly generated, as well as the wind direction, circle placement, and the size of the particles.
